Returns & Exchanges.

We want every 444 Store piece to fit right the first time. If it doesn't, here's exactly how our returns and exchange process works.

Eligibility Window

Items may be returned or exchanged within 14 days of the delivery date shown on your dispatch notification. Requests made after this window cannot be accepted.

Condition Requirements

Pieces must be unworn, unwashed and returned with all original tags and packaging attached. Items showing signs of wear, odour, or alteration will not be accepted and will be sent back to you.

How To Start A Return

Message us on WhatsApp or through our contact form with your order number and reason for return. We'll arrange a free pickup within Accra, or share drop-off instructions for other regions.

Refunds

Approved returns are refunded to your original payment method within 5–7 business days of us receiving the item. Mobile Money refunds are typically faster. Delivery fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Exchanges

Prefer a different size or colour? Exchanges are free within the 14-day window, subject to stock availability. We'll dispatch your replacement as soon as your original item is received and inspected.

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene reasons, headwear and undergarments cannot be returned unless faulty. Final sale / Archive items marked as such at checkout are also excluded from returns.
